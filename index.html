Roles

We have multiple roles for our community

Archons

The Seven Archons, typically shortened to The Seven, are the seven gods who preside over the seven regions of Teyvat. Each Archon is associated with an element and an ideal, by which they formed their territories' environment and determined their method of governance over their regions.

Travelers

The Traveler eventually wakes up after an undisclosed amount of time and proceeds to wander across Teyvat. They are accompanied by a travel companion named Paimon throughout their journey. They get in contact with The Seven in order to help the Traveler look for their lost power.

Outlanders

Same as the Traveler, Outlanders come from afar world of Teyvat.
